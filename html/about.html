ABOUT

Bread Beacon aims to provide an easy way for college students to navigate all the resources available to access healthy food and find connection with helpful local people.

With the use of Bread Beacon, we hope students will be able to take the first steps in resolving their food-related challenges.

Feel free to contact us using the form below if you have any questions, suggestions and/or comments!
